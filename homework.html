Top Rated

Greece - Santorini

Montenegro - Budva

Santorini is the most popular Greek destination and a worldwide famous island! Once you set your foot in Santorini, you’ll feel like entering a whole new world
Bodrum is one of the most chic and European style resorts in Turkey, fantastic beaches, white coloured houses, secret coves, fishing villages and trendy nightclubs.
The Budva Riviera has some of the most attractive beaches of south Adriatic, and the most pleasant climate in Montenegro. Budva is 2,500 years old, which makes it one of the oldest settlements on the Adriatic coast.
Top Rated

Bulgaria - Golden sands

Albania - Valona

Croatia - Split

Golden Sands is a major seaside resort town on the northern Bulgarian Black Sea Coast, adjacent to a national park of the same name in the municipality of Varna. Located 17 km north of downtown Varna, it is virtually connected to the city by a continuous swath of resorts and villa communities.
Tourism has become a major industry in recent years, with many hotels, recreational centers, and vast beaches. The city has a good view over the Bay of Vlorë, which is considered the frontier between the Adriatic Sea and the Ionian Sea.
Split is Croatia's second-largest city and the largest city in the Dalmatia region. It lies on the eastern shore of the Adriatic Sea and is spread over a central peninsula and its surroundings.
